About Me

My name is Enrique Cruz Montealegre III. I am most commonly referred to as Novel.

I am a Digital Media Creator. I produce content through the use of editing software. I specialize in multimedia content creation. I have been fascinated by the world of editing and content creation ever since I was little. I casually watch people on the internet create special Visual Effects (VFX) with the use of video editing software, as well as watching people create artworks with the use of image manipulation programs.

The ability to create something to conjure an entirely different thing. Digital media is a beautiful artform that amalagamates the creativy of the human mind and the capabilities of technology together.

In my years of growing up I would always experiment with tutorials on numerous sites such as YouTube and with the little equipment I had back then, I made my own content for fun. Whenever I come up with an idea, I pursue to create it just for my own leisure.
Now being a student of the Creative Computing course, along with my past experiences as a novice editor. I compiled some of my creative works within this website.
