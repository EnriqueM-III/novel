Graphic Design


The community, Rise of the Zodiacs. Is a Text-Based Roleplay community where its story is inspired by famed Japanese manga and anime, Black Clover. Having their own original story and worldbuilding.

Aside from being a member of this community and writing alongside them. I was happy to be involved with the community to add something special to the members of the community. I conceptualized their vision of their character's Grimoire. (A magic book that contains spells)

I am simply given a material as to what their character's abilities are, specific symbols, color scheme. etc. to work with and show them the results. Making use of editing programs along with saved images to work on, I was able to create book covers that compliment their descriptions.

I took the leisure in producing these works for the sole purpose of practicing my skills in compositioning, blending colors and effects, color correction, layers, everything related to image editing to create an appropriate book cover that defines the description that was given to me. Every one that commissioned a piece were more than satisfied with the results and it is an overall amazing experience for me as an aspiring creator.

Video Editing


This field of multimedia creation is the one I am most experienced in. Video Editing started out as a hobby for me at a young age and it now has become a skill asset that I use on a professional basis.

From splicing video footage to visual effects, I have a very strong affinity for enhancing my knowledge in video editing even further. Learning different techniques such as color grading and 3D visuals, experiencing different types of learning styles while I'm in the process. For my programs I use the Adobe Creative Suite set of programs such as Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Photoshop.

Audio Mixing


This area of content creation is something I just started recently and it has been growing as one of my newer hobbies, Audio Mixing is a skillset that is very interesting as to how artists get their music to be so well made and pleasing to the ears. As a person who is in love with music as a whole, this skillset in content creation is going to be a fresh and new start for me personally.

I use a Digital Audio Workstation called Reaper. It is a free DAW software that has the same capabilities of a professional Digital Audio Workstation. I've experimented into mixing music by making music covers to mix . This serves as a starting point and added experience in audio content creation.
